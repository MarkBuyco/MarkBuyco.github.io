Background

It all started with an idea and love. We started our online business back in 2022 because of the love for thrifting. At one point in time, I thrifted 5 shirts, that is when Kicks and Drip was born. Just the idea of thrifting thrills me, finding the right one in the pile of clothes. Aside from thrifting, We love shoes, hence the "kicks" in our business name. Collecting pairs of shoes has always been our hobby, we am what they call a 'sneakerhead.'


Goal

Our goal to help other people like me to do what they love, thrifting. We want to help in a way that thrifting would be easier for our costumers as they will choose clothes and shoes online rather than going to a physical store. It saves time, money, and effort.


Vision

We want to give our costumers fashionable and trendy clothes that doesn't cost an arm and a leg. In 5 years, our vision is to expand out business across the United Arab Emirates. We want to grow and have more in our team, but the heart will always be there, the heart for loving clothes and shoes. The passion will never change in Kicks & Drip.
